Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 24, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to AI App (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the App. The App is a personal video upload automation tool designed for my individual use. I respect your privacy and am committed to protecting it.

Key Point: This App is a personal tool designed for my individual use. I do not operate a commercial service. The App does not collect, store, or share user data beyond what is necessary for the core functionality.

2. Information I Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • TikTok Account Information: When you authenticate with TikTok via OAuth 2.0, I receive basic account information (user ID and display name) to identify your TikTok account.
  • Video Content: Video files (MP4 format) that you select for upload to TikTok.
  • Video Metadata: Titles, descriptions, and tags that you enter for each video.

2.2 Information Automatically Collected

  • No automatic data collection: The App does not use cookies, analytics trackers, or any automated data collection mechanisms.
  • No logging: The App does not log your IP address, browser type, device information, or usage patterns.

3. How I Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: To authenticate your TikTok account via OAuth 2.0.
  • Video Publishing: To upload and publish videos to your TikTok account using the Content Posting API.
  • Status Updates: To display upload progress and video URL to you.

I do not use your information for any other purpose, including marketing, analytics, or profiling.

4. How I Share Your Information

I do not share, sell, rent, or trade your information with any third parties. The only disclosures are:

  • TikTok API: Your information is transmitted to TikTok's servers solely for the purpose of authentication and video publishing, in accordance with TikTok's Privacy Policy.
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law or in response to a valid legal request (e.g., court order, subpoena), I may be required to disclose information.

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Data Storage

  • No permanent storage: The App does not store your videos, metadata, or account information after the upload is complete.
  • Temporary processing: Video files are processed only during the upload session and are not retained after the API response is received.
  • OAuth tokens: Authentication tokens are used only for the current session and are not persisted.

5.2 Data Security

I take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Secure transmission via HTTPS/TLS
  • Use of secure OAuth 2.0 flows
  • No local storage of sensitive data

6. Your Rights and Choices

Because the App does not store your data, there is no data to access, correct, or delete. However, you have the following rights:

  • Revoke Access: You can revoke the App's access to your TikTok account at any time via your TikTok account settings.
  • Stop Using the App: You may stop using the App at any time. No data will be retained.
  • Contact Me: If you have any privacy concerns, you can contact me using the information in Section 10.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). I do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through the App, please contact me so I can take appropriate action.
